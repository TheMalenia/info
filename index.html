👋 About Me

I'm Hasan, a 20-year-old with a mind wired for math, programming, and all things problem-solving. I love breaking things down, finding patterns, and pushing my brain to think in new ways-whether through algorithmic programming, pure math, or even a good puzzle game.

Back in high school, I was all in on the Informatics Olympiad, spending my time deep in combinatorics and algorithmic problem-solving. That world still fascinates me. But university took me in a slightly different direction-I ended up studying Mathematics and Applications at Shahid Beheshti University. At first, it wasn't what I had planned, but over time, I realized how much I enjoy the depth and structure of mathematics. It's a different kind of challenge, one that constantly reshapes the way I think.

Programming, though, has always been a core part of me. From C++ to Python, I've explored different areas, but lately, Django has been my main focus. I even joined a Django bootcamp at Quera, diving deeper into the world of web development.

Beyond math and coding, I have a soft spot for podcasts, especially ones about tech, philosophy, and deep-dive storytelling. I also love games-whether it's classics like Zelda and Zork or exploring the world of Bitcoin and decentralized systems. And then there's art-music, writing, painting-things I've never fully pursued but always appreciated from the sidelines.

That's me, always exploring, always learning. 🚀
